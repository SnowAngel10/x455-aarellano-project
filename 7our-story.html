Our Story

Jane and Sarah’s passion for flowers began at a young age. They both grew up in homes with lush gardens and spent many hours helping their parents tend to the plants. As they got older, they pursued their interest in floral design by taking courses and attending workshops. However, they found that many of these resources were expensive and inaccessible to those on a budget. This led them to start their DIY flower blog, with the goal of providing affordable and accessible resources for others who share their passion for flowers. Over time, their blog has grown to become a source of inspiration for many, and they continue to share their love for gardening and flower arranging with their followers. For Jane and Sarah, Pink Petals is more than just a website – it’s a community of like-minded individuals who share their love for all things floral.
